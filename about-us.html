General Information


The Regents Scholar Society is an organization comprised of the top 1.5% - judged holistically - applicants at UCLA. Our scholars are some of the brightest, most passionate, and most hard-working students at UCLA! Along with that, the Regents Scholar Society provides various benefits to its members, listed below. Along with that, our society provides a social network: there's always an event coming up or someone interesting to meet!

Our activities include:

    • - Social: Kayaking, Downtown LA adventures, food excursions, and more!
      - Academic: Peer, faculty, and alumni mentor programs, skills workshops!
      - Professional: Faculty and alumni networking events, resume reviews, etc.!



    • BENEFITS OF BEING REGENTS


    Being a Regents Scholars is not without its perks. As a Regents Scholar at UCLA, you get:

    Honorarium: a $2,000 merit scholarship is given to all recipients of the Regents Scholarship.

    Priority Enrollment: when signing up for courses, Regents Scholars are able to enroll with a "priority" time - meaning you will get to enroll before all students at UCLA, besides other priority time holders.

    Guaranteed 4-year Housing: as long as you fill out all applicable forms and make all payments on time, all Regents Scholars receive a 4-year guarantee for on-campus housing.

    Guaranteed Parking: as long as forms are filled out and payments are made in time, all scholars are guaranteed to be accepted for an on-campus parking permit.

    Society Membership: all scholarship recipients are automatically members of the Regents Scholar Society, meaning you have access to all of our academic, professional, and logistical resources.
